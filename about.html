Our Story

In the early 2010s, the founder of Thought Electric, Inc. was working as an apprentice electrician in Los Angeles, honing his skills on various residential and commercial projects. While he was passionate about the technical aspects of electrical work, he began to notice a growing demand for smarter, more energy-efficient solutions in homes and businesses throughout Southern California. This realization sparked an ideaâ€”what if there was a company that combined traditional electrical services with cutting-edge technology, making modern living accessible to everyone?

In 2015, with that inspiration in mind, Thought Electric, Inc. was born in a small garage in Orange County. The founder, driven by a desire to make a difference, started by offering basic electrical services to local homeowners. As word spread about his meticulous work and innovative approach, the business quickly grew. The company expanded its services to include smart home installations, energy-efficient lighting, and advanced electrical systems, helping clients transform their homes and businesses into modern, efficient spaces.

As the company continued to grow, the founder recognized the need for a more robust infrastructure to support its expanding operations. In 2019, Thought Electric, Inc. moved into a larger facility in Orange County, equipped with state-of-the-art tools and equipment to handle larger projects and accommodate the increasing demand for its services. The company also expanded its team, hiring skilled electricians and technicians who shared the same passion for innovation and customer satisfaction.

Over the years, Thought Electric, Inc. has remained committed to its roots while continuously adapting to the ever-evolving technology landscape. The company's mission has always been to provide high-quality electrical services with a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction. Today, Thought Electric, Inc. serves the greater Los Angeles and Orange County areas, proud to be a part of the community and dedicated to bringing the best in electrical solutions to its clients.
